Our Company History

Established in 2016 E.C., E Laundry was founded by Ermias Mazengia with a clear mission: to bring professional, reliable, and affordable laundry services to the heart of the community. What began as a modest laundry shop has grown into a respected and trusted name in the industry.

Mr. Ermias Mazengia started E Laundry with just a few machines, a small team, and a big vision to simplify peopleâ€™s lives by taking the hassle out of laundry. With dedication to cleanliness, customer care, and attention to every detail, E Laundry quickly built a loyal customer base.

As the demand for modern and convenient laundry services increased, E Laundry expanded its offerings, including:

  • Wash and fold
  • Dry cleaning
  • Ironing and pressing
  • Stain removal and fabric care
  • Pickup and delivery services
  • Commercial laundry for hotels, restaurants, and clinics

From day one, we have focused on providing high-quality service with a personal touch. Over the years, we have invested in modern laundry equipment, eco-friendly practices, and staff training to maintain the highest standards in the business.

Today, E Laundry proudly serves individuals, families, and businesses across Addis Ababa, making laundry easier, faster, and more reliable than ever. As we continue to grow, our commitment remains the same: clean clothes, happy customers, and a cleaner future.
